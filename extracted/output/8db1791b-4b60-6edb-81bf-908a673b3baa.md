This method is meant to be used in conjunction with [FindEnclosingRegionAndSegments(UV, RectangularGridSegmentOrientation, Int32%, Int32%)](e5a63d60-6fa3-f414-20b5-ef72da637849.md). Grid coordinates correspond to uv coordinates of faces.
